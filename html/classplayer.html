Manages interaction between vehicles.

Checks whether the player vehicle overlaps with the oncoming vehicles using the overlap and within functions it inherited from car Should the player have enough lives to survive the collision then their lives will go down by one, the oncoming car's position will be reset and the player can continue playing. If the player doesn't have enough lives left, they get a GAME OVER screen, their score is shown and the game exits If the player vehicle doesn't collide with anything then the score goes up
